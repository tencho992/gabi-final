Fine Line Tattoo/ Minimal

Experience the beauty of delicate and intricate designs with our fine line tattooing service. Gabriela specializes in creating stunning, detailed tattoos using thin, precise lines. Whether you're looking for a minimalistic design or a small, intricate piece, Gabi will bring your vision to life with her expertise and attention to detail. Embrace the elegance of fine line tattoos and make a subtle yet powerful statement.

Complex Tattoos

Unleash your imagination and let Gabi bring your wildest tattoo ideas to reality! Our complex tattoo service is designed to handle intricate and detailed designs, no matter how elaborate or challenging they may be. From large-scale pieces to multi-layered artwork, we thrive on pushing the boundaries of creativity. Trust Gabi to capture your unique story and transform it into a breathtaking masterpiece that you'll cherish forever.

Piercings

Gabi provides a wide range of piercing options, from ear piercings to body piercings, all performed with meticulous care and precision. A safe and hygienic environment for all your piercing needs. Choose from a variety of jewelry options and let Gabi create a stunning look that complements your personality and style.
